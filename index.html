About us

“Quality is what we guarantee.”

In a world that moves ever so swiftly, Wisteria & Co. remains devoted to the gentle art of floristry where blossoms speak in place of words, and every arrangement is crafted with care, sentiment, and grace. Nestled in Valenzuela City, we are an online flower delivery shop serving the cherished corners of Metro Manila such as Caloocan, Las Piñas, Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Manila, Marikina, Muntinlupa, Navotas, Parañaque, Pasay, Pasig, Pateros, Quezon City, San Juan, and Taguig, as well as select nearby provinces of Rizal, Cavite, and Laguna.
Our offerings are a reflection of timeless beauty, hand-tied bouquets, round arrangements, and stately flower stands for solemn farewells and grand occasions alike. We also present floral baskets, vases, and boxed creations, each thoughtfully arranged in the spirit of elegance and tradition.
For those wishing to add a tender touch, we offer delightful keepsakes such as chocolates, teddy bears, and balloons as simple tokens that carry warmth and affection. At Wisteria & Co., we hold dear the belief that flowers are not merely gifts, but gentle messengers of the heart. With every stem and ribbon, we remain faithful to quality, sincerity, and the enduring charm of giving.
